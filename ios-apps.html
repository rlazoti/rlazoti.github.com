iSpeedLight

  • What does it do?

    iSpeedLight is a new way to control the speed of your car, motorcycle, bicycle and others. You'll have a digital speedometer and a speed monitor on your device screen. This application requires that the GPS of your iPhone is activated.

    How does it work?

    Monitor your speed using the iSpeedLight, determine two warnings (yellow light and red light) for your attention speed and maximum speed.

    • White light indicates that the device is not receiving a signal from the GPS.
    • Green light indicates that the device is on speed limits.
    • Yellow light indicates that the device passed the attention speed determined.
    • Red light indicates that the device passed the maximum speed determined.

    Available Language

    English and Portuguese

    Download the iPhone Version on App Store
