Hi! I’m Rodrigo Lazoti, a software developer based in Sao Paulo, Brazil, where I am currently working as a Technical Lead at HOTWords.

I am passionate about technology in general and especially in programming languages and open source technologies, and I've been developed applications using Delphi, Asp, Php, Ruby, Java, Javascript, Objective-C and Scala.

I like to learn new things about programming languages, Unix-like operating systems, iOS, and open source technologies, so I usually try to write about those things in this blog.

I’m also venturing into the world of applications for iOS Platform and I already have some applications available for purchase in the App Store.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Computer Information Systems Degree from College Drummond and I also have a Postgraduate diploma in Software Engineer from PUC-MG (Pontifícia Universidade Católica de Minas Gerais).
